Broccoli Cheddar Souop

A comfort food classic! Serve with your favorite bread.


Ingredients

  • 1/2 sweet onion
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 3 carrots
  • 3 cups trimmed broccoli
  • 4 tbsp vegan butter
  • 4 tbsp chickpea flour
  • 2 cups light cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated cheddar cheese
  • 2 tsp seasoned salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp Better Than Bouillon
  • 2 cups hot water

Method

  1. Dice 1/2 a sweet onion and mince 5 gloves of fresh garlic
  2. Peel and shred 3 carrots. Trim and prepare 3 cups of broccoli.
  3. Melt 4 tbsp of vegan butter in a deep pan. Once melted, add the onion and garlic and cook down until fragrent.
  4. Add 2 tsp seasoned salt, 1 tsp garlic powder, and 1 tsp paprika to the pot. Mix until the onion is fully coated in the spices.
  5. Add 4 tbsp chickpea flour and stir until dissolved in the pan.
  6. In a separate bowl/Pyrex, add 2 tsp Better Than Bouillon to 2 cups of hot water and stir until dissolved. Add the water to the pot and stir.
  7. Reduce the heat and cover the pot. Allow the mixture to simmer for 5 minutes or until thickened.
  8. Add the shredded carrots, broccoli, and 2 cups of light cream. Stir to incorporate.
  9. Reduce heat and simmer the mixture for 15 minutes on medium low heat.
  10. Add 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese to complete the soup. Stir in the cheese and add any additional desired spices to taste.
