Cinnamon Bread

This bread is perfect for an autumn or winter treat.


Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 4 tbsp melted unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 egg wash

Method

  1. In a medium bowl, add the flour, salt, sugar, and yeast. Lightly mix.
  2. Combine the warm water and vanilla in a separate bowl/cup. Mix the two and add it to the dry ingredients.
  3. Add the melted butter and mix until a dough is formed. Knead the dough until smooth.
  4. Place the dough in a bowl and cover with a tea towel. Prove for 1 hour or until the dough has roughly doubled in size.
  5. Divide the dough into quarters and allow the four dough balls to rest for 10 minutes.
  6. Combine the light brown sugar and cinnamon in a separate small bowl to make the filling.
  7. Roll out each dough ball until thin and flat. Brush on egg wash and add an even layer of filling. Cover with the next oval of dough and repeat until the fourth layer is added.
  8. Slice roughly four inches from the outside of the circle to form 16 even strands. Place a small bowl or cup in the middle of the loaf to protect the center while cutting.
  9. Twist each strand in the opposite direction of the one beside it to form a twisted point. Press the ends together to form a thicker petal.
  10. Egg wash the entire exterior of the loaf. Bake at 400F for 15 minutes.
